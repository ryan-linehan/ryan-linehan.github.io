Tribal Trouble

Classic RTS Revival

Tribal Trouble was originally released in 2004 and later open sourced under the GPL2 license in 2014. There was a Tribal Trouble 2 at one point, but it was web-based and pay-to-play, and has since been taken offline.

Several attempts have been made to revive it, but they were usually "closed" where the code wasn't shared. Now we are reviving the game and keeping everything open while modernizing it for a new generation of players.

What We're Adding

  • Modernized graphics
  • Steam release
  • More players in one game
  • Multiple islands on one map
  • Boats
  • Online replays
  • Discord bot integrations
  • Quality of life improvements
LANGUAGE Java
LICENSE GPL2
ORIGINAL 2004

Shoot'em & Scoot'em

Physics Puzzle Platformer

A physics puzzle platformer inspired by Cut the Rope. You are a sheriff space cowboy who uses his pistol to fly, shoot, and scoot through the air to reach your trusty steed Scooty.

Use your limited number of bullets to get to your horse while shooting aliens. Each alien is worth a star, and there are three stars to get on each level!

Game Features

  • 60 levels of physics-based puzzles
  • 3 unique environments: Spaceship, Asteroid Belt, Alien Planet
  • Limited bullets = strategic gameplay
  • Star collection challenges
  • Space cowboy aesthetic
GENRE Puzzle Platformer
LEVELS 60
ENVIRONMENTS 3
